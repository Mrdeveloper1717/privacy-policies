Privacy Policy

Developer: Kitoblar Olami (Sadirboyprogrammer)
This Privacy Policy applies to all mobile applications published by the developer.

Kitoblar Olami develops multiple free book-related mobile applications. These apps are provided at no cost and are intended for use as-is. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled across all of our applications.

Information Collection and Use

Our applications do not collect, store, or require any personally identifiable information such as name, email address, phone number, or account credentials.

Some of our apps display advertisements using third-party services such as Google AdMob. These services may collect anonymous data (such as the device’s Advertising ID) for ad delivery, frequency capping, and performance measurement.

The developer does not have access to, collect, or store this advertising data. All ad-related data processing is governed by Google’s own Privacy Policy.

Permissions Used

Depending on the application, the following permissions may be used:

  • INTERNET — to load advertisements or online content.
  • ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE — to check network connectivity.
  • com.google.android.gms.permission.AD_ID — for advertising purposes (personalized or non-personalized ads).

Data Security

We do not collect or store user data on our servers. Any data processed by third-party services is handled securely under their respective policies.

Children’s Privacy

Our applications do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe that a child has provided personal data through a third-party service, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.
